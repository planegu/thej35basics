The SAAB J-35 Draken

You may know the J-35 as the first aircraft that was able of pulling of the cobra manouver, which is pretty interesting in itself but there are way more things to find out.

Development

  • The aircraft was developed by the swedish aeronautical company SAAB ( Svenska Aeroplan Aktiebolaget)
  • The development started in 1948 in order to replace the aging J-29 and J-32
  • The aircraft was developed from the Fpl 210
  • The Draken made it's first flight on the 25th of October in 1955
  • It's maximum operational altitude is 20.000 metres

Capabilities

  • The aircraft can reach speeds of Mach 2
  • It can do the infamous "Cobra" manouver
  • It's maximum operational ceeling is 20.000 metres
  • It has the range of 3250 kilometres

Armament(all variants)

  • The fighter has 2 30mm canons with 200 rounds of ammo in total
  • When it comes to air-to-air missiles it can carry AIM-9B Sidewinder and AIM-9J Sidewinde
  • For air-to-ground misiles the jet can be equiped with the RB 05 AGM.

Operators

  • Swedish Air Force
  • Finnish Air Force
  • Austrian Air Force
